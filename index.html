03 ‚Äî Hypothesis-Driven Design

Calm Banking ‚Äî Does Visual Restraint Build More Trust?

I started with a hypothesis: calm, minimal interfaces build more trust than dense, engagement-optimised ones. Designed 5 core banking screens using semantic-only colour, strict 8px grid, and intentional negative space. Validated with 5 participants ‚Äî all 5 preferred the restrained design over a feature-rich alternative. Sometimes the right answer is knowing what not to design.
